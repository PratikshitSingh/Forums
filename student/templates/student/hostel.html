About Us

Named after the great Indian astronomer and mathematician, Aryabhatta, this is the largest hostel in the IIT BHU campus. It was inaugurated on 24th July 2012.

Aryabhatta hostel is divided into two parts, each consisting of two blocks. Blocks A and B belong to Aryabhatta 1, which in total has 230 rooms accommodating above 450 students while Aryabhatta 2 consists of block C and D , which in total has 198 rooms accommodating over 350 students.Â  This hostel comprises of a large common room, big sized rooms, three canteens and four beautiful gardens. With a strength of over 800 students this is one of the liveliest hostels in IIT BHU.

Dr. Santosh Kumar is the Admin Warden of Aryabhatta 1 and Dr. Mohd. Z.K. Yousufzai is the Admin Warden of Aryabhatta 2.

Rules and Regulations

The disciplinary committee shall consist of the Warden in-charge, Associate Wardens, Hostel Secretary, one student nominated by the Warden in-charge and one student nominated by the general body. The disciplinary committee shall take action against the students who will violate the code of conduct of the hostel.
